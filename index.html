Questions

The questions you're
already asking.

Why are your prices premium?+

Cheap creative is the most expensive mistake a brand can make. Our pricing reflects the business outcomes we're accountable to — not hours logged in a spreadsheet. Every engagement is structured to compound brand equity, not just produce content.

Why choose Mcube over a larger agency?+

At a large agency, your brand is one account among hundreds. At Mcube, the founder is directly involved in every engagement. You work with senior creative and strategic minds, not account managers relaying feedback through layers.

How long does a project take?+

Brand identity projects typically run 6–10 weeks. Campaign launches take 3–4 weeks from brief to live. Monthly retainers operate on a rolling creative calendar built around your business objectives, not ours.

Do you work internationally?+

Yes. Mcube is based in Coimbatore and works with clients across India and internationally. Our processes are built for remote collaboration, and our creative output is designed for global markets.

Do you offer AI services?+

Yes — our AI Studio produces commercials, product photography, fashion model imagery, and voiceovers at a fraction of conventional production cost. Every AI output is held to the same creative direction and quality standard as our traditional production work.

Do you take one-off projects or only retainers?+

Both. One-off projects include brand identity, website design, and commercial film production. Retainers suit brands building compounding brand equity over time. We'll advise on what fits your current stage.
